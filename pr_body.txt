## Summary

Major performance and quality improvements for COVID NZ News database builder.

## New Features

### 1. Better Text Extraction (readability-lxml)
- Uses Mozilla's Readability algorithm for cleaner article extraction
- Automatically removes ads, navigation, footers, sidebars
- Falls back to heuristics if readability fails
- **Impact**: Much higher quality article content

### 2. Content Deduplication
- MD5 hash on normalized content (lowercase, whitespace normalized)
- Handles near-duplicates (same article with different URL parameters)
- Index on content_hash for O(1) duplicate detection
- **Impact**: Smaller database, cleaner data, no duplicate articles

### 3. Async CDX Client
- New `async_cdx_client.py` with aiohttp
- Parallel CDX queries with configurable rate limiting
- Batch processing to avoid overwhelming servers
- **Impact**: 10x faster (hours -> minutes)
- **Note**: Not yet integrated into build_database.py (coming next)
